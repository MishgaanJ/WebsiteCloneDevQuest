ABOUT US

Technokrates is a start-up company founded by a mechanical engineer, who is passionate about developing innovative products that make processes easier and more efficient.
As a company, Technokrates is committed to sustainability and eco-friendliness , and Technokrates strive to create products that are not only useful, but also have a minimal impact on the environment.







“Technokrates is the go-to source for innovative and sustainable solutions, offering durable, cost-effective eco bricks that are better for the environment.”







WHERE WE STAND

Technokrates is seeking partners to help bring our innovative Eco Bricks to market. These bricks are a sustainable alternative to traditional pavers, made from recycled plastic. They are more durable, cost-effective, and eco friendly, and come in three types: City, Uni Lock, and High Lock.
By partnering with us, you can not only help us bring our product to market, but also contribute to a more sustainable future. We have developed a prototype and are now seeking investors to help us build the machinery necessary for production.
If you are interested in learning more about Technokrates and how you can get involved, we encourage you to contact us.
